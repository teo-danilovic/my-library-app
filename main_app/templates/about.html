About myLibrary

myLibrary is a digital catalog and management tool designed for avid readers and book enthusiasts.

Our main purposes are to:

  • Maintain a Database of Books: Allow users to Create, Read, Update, and Delete (CRUD) entries for individual books, storing details like title, author, genre, publication year, and reading status.
  • Track Reading Progress: Enable users to log and view reading sessions or progress (date started, date finished, current page, personal rating, notes) for each book.
  • Manage a Tag Inventory: Allow users to maintain a list of tags for categorizing books.
  • Link Tags to Books: Provide a way to associate specific tags from the inventory with particular books.
